Person of the Week

Jason Pamental is a strategist, designer, technologist, and author of Responsive Typography from O’Reilly. Having worked on the web since 1994 in a range of capacities, he’s an overall web and typography nerd who specializes in being a generalist. Jason has led both creative and technical efforts for major universities, NFL and America’s Cup teams, Fortune 25 corporations and many others. He gives presentations and workshops at conferences all over the world and publishes a newsletter on web typography. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Jason!
